GATES OF GALOO
"I CALL IT THE GATES OF GALOO"
  • Highlight Video
  • GitHub Repository
  • Platformer, RPG, Fantasy

The "Gates of Galoo" was my first full-game prototype learning project. It was a third person platformer that featured a large level full of dangerous enemies to fight and valuable treasures to find.

Gameplay consisted of traversing terrain and floating platforms while battling enemies in order to find coins, weapons, health pickups, and ultimately the key to the end of the level.

I spent roughly 150 hours on this project, and learned how to code many features, including (but not limited to):

Moving Platforms - Floor Switches - Enemies - Melee Combat - Collision & Collision Channels - Weapons - Sockets - Spawning Actors & Spawn Volumes - Trigger Volumes - Pickups - Hazards - Stamina & Stamina Drain Sprinting - Crouching - "Lock On" Function - Save & Load System - Binding HUD Widgets & Widget Animations - Weapon Trails - Adding Force & Torque - Cameras and Springarms -
