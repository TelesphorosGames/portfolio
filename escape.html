Graveyard Escape was made for the 2022 Gamedev.Tv Game Jam in 10 days as a solo dev according to the theme, "Death is only the beginning."

Players took the role of a zombie trying to escape from a graveyard by hiding behind the objects in the scene and finding bushes that temorarily shielded them from the guards' line of sight.

The game was rated in the 90th percentile of all submisisons, and I was extremely thrilled to have such positive reviews. Features that I implemented in this game included:

AI Sight Detection - AI Patrol and Search - Invisibility System - Overlaps and Trigger Volumes - Blueprinting Mechanics - Level Design - Animation Retargeting & Blueprinting - Stamina & Stamina Drain - Game Difficulties - Basic Settings - Cinematic Start Menu - Pause Menu
