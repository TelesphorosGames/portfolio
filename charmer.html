Wil Wilson: Worm Charmer was my entry to the 2022 Epic Megajam. I made the game in 7 days as a solo dev according to the theme, "As above, so below."

Players took the role of Wil as they tried to help him collect worms for his paw. Gameplay consisted of lighthearted roleplaying in a friendly town, and a mini-game requiring players to press the correct button at the correct time.

I implemented a lot of the things I've learned throughout the course of my studies and was very happy to have successfully entered the jam. Some of the features the game included were:

Post Process Materials - Box Traces - Blueprinting Mechanics - Widget Dialog Boxes - Level Design - Start and Pause Menus - Randomly Moving Objects - Animation Retargeting & Blueprinting - Material Editing - Particle Effect Manipulation
