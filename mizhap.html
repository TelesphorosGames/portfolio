"miZhap" was a third-person shooter game prototype where the player took the role of one of the last survivors in a burning city during a zombie plague.

Gameplay consisted of shooting through hordes of enemies, having to move through dark areas, and gathering weapons, ammo, and resources.

I chose to move away from this project although I would have loved to spend more months working on it. The features I learned to implement while working on it included:

Turn In Place - Aim Offsets - Dynamic Crosshairs - Automatic Fire - Weapon Types - Glowing Outline Effects - Inventory System - Data Tables - Animation Retargeting ( UE4 & 5 ) - Floating Damage Numbers - Hit Boxes - Inverse Kinematics - Widget Blueprints - Binding Widget Animations - Item Interpolation - Curves & Animation Curves - Footstep Notifies - Physical Surface Types - Interfaces - Physics Assets - Custom Behavior Tree Tasks
